GAMEPLAY RULES

  1. Level-Easy consists of only 2 colors whereas, Level-Hard contains 4 colors
  2. On entering the game you will see the ball at the bottom of the page.
  3. On tapping the screen the ball would bounce up and drop down.
  4. The main Objective of the game is to bounce and move up the screen.
  5. The distance the ball moves up, your score increases gradually
  6. To gain a score of 1 you need to move up to cross the Checkpoints
  7. Each Checkpoints are denoted by a star
  8. On moving up you would face a lot of obstacles and you need to cross them
  9. As you move up to great distances the speed of the obstacles you face would gradually increase (by a factor of 1.05)
  10. On the way, you would face many Color Switch balls which changes your ball color
  11. You have to be careful during the color switch

    12. BE THE BEST OF ALL BY BREAKING THE HIGHSCORE
